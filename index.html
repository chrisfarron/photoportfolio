CHRIS FARRON

ABOUT ME

I was born and raised in the Mission District of San Francisco, CA where there has seemingly always been a flourishing tight knit Art community. I graduated from the University of California, Santa Cruz where I received my Bachelor of Arts degree in the History of Art & Visual Culture and a minor degree in Applied Art, emphasizing in Photography. I enjoy producing, experiencing and writing about art, photography, architecture, music and film.

Upon my graduation from UC Santa Cruz, I worked as a Visual Merchandiser for The North Face based here in the Bay Area. This allowed me to exercise my creativity in elevating the shopping experience for customers in The North Face retail stores as well as using the setting of a store as a marketing vehicle to convey our messages and values.

Most recently, I worked an Assistant Buyer for Sears Holding at their apparel office here in San Francisco, where I primarily focused on building and maintaining relationships with vendors, product management, advertising strategies and retail analytics.
